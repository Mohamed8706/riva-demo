**A**: PostgreSQL recommended (see BACKEND_GUIDE.md line 17)

**A**: 
1. Create component in `/src/app/pages/`
2. Add route in `/src/app/App.tsx`
3. Add navigation link in Navbar or Dashboard

### Q: How do I change colors?

- `from-sky-500` ‚Üí your primary color
